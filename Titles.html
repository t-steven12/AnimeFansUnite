Titles

The Titles page regulates the Titles table.

This page displays a table of all available anime titles and their associated artists.
An anime title can be added by submitting the anime title and the name of the artist.
The artists in the Artists column can be updated by pressing the UPDATE button next to
the desired row to be updated. The relationship between Titles and Artists is NULLable.
The associated Artist of a Title can be NULL upon insertion and update.
